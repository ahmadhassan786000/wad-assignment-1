Introduction

The Department of Computer Sciences at Quaid-i-Azam University is among the best in Pakistan. Ranked top 3 nationally by QS and THE rankings, it was founded in 1976. The department aims to produce top-tier computer professionals and researchers. Graduates work internationally and locally, and the department offers PhD, MPhil, MS (IST), MS (Data Science), and BS programs.
