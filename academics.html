Academics

The department offers PhD, MPhil, MS (Information Science & Technology), M.Sc. and BS degrees. Each degree programme has a specific objective and focus. The course of study and syllabi for each degree is updated and is in line with its objectives.

  • PhD is the highest degree awarded in an academic discipline. Students work closely with a faculty member, performing original research and tackling unsolved problems.
  • more details about program
  • The MS program focuses on the collaborative environment of how people and society interact with digital information. Open for BS or M.Sc. graduates with relevant experience.
  • more details about program
  • The M.Phil program trains students to initiate research work. It spans 2 years (4 semesters) with a total of 50 credit hours combining coursework and research.
  • more details about program
  • The MS Data Science program meets the need for analyzing increasing data from multiple sources and equips students with current knowledge in the field.
  • more details about program
  • The BS Computer Science program offers a balanced mix of theory and practical skills in software development, computational systems, communication, and multimedia.
