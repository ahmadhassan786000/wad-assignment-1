MPil (computer science)

Department of Computer Sciences

about
  • The objective of M.Phil Computer Science programme is to train students to initiate research work. The duration of M.Phil programme is two years (4 semesters).. MPhil is of 50 credit hours, 24 credit hours for the course work and 26 credit hours for research work. Each course is of 3 credit hours. There are 2 compulsory courses, and 6 optional courses are taken to complete 24 credits. The courses are designed in such a way that during the course work students will be exposed to research activities. The course work will set the basic ground work to start research.Students will register for a research topic in the 3rd semester and will complete the research work by the end of the 4th semester. The research work will be submitted in the form of a thesis
  • Dept. of Computer Science, Quaid-i-Azam University, Islamabad, Pakistan.

Admission requirements
  • Students will be selected on the basis of scholarship and research potential among applicants with appropriate degrees from HEC recognized university as per university rules.
  • minimum requirements
  • M.Sc.(Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Technology) or
  • 4 years BS (Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Technology)
  • Must pass test (sample test available) and interview arranged by the department
  • sample test
program structure
  • Students will register for a research topic in the 3rd semester and will complete the research work by the end of the 4th semester.
  • The research work will be submitted in the form of a thesis.
  • 50 credit hours
    • 24 credit hours for course work (2 compulsory courses and 6 optional courses are taken to complete 24 credits)
    • 6 credit hours for research work

FAQs
  • When will MPhil admissions open? Admissions open in Fall semester for every academic year.
  • What is the eligibility criteria? MSc/BS (4 years) in CS/SE/IT from the HEC recognized university.
  • What will be the format of MPhil admission test? Sample admission test is provided at http://qau.edu.pk/tests/CSC-mphil-test.pdf
  • What will be the duration of admission test? Duration of admission test will be 90 to 120 minutes.
  • How can I apply? You can apply online by visiting the university website http://ugadmissions.qau.edu.pk/
  • Can I study part time? No, it’s a full-time program, with most of the classes in the morning.
  • What is the fee structure? To know more about fee structure, please visit http://qau.edu.pk/mphil-programme-fee-structure
  • What is the duration/credit hours requirement of MPhil? MPhil is a two-year program of 50 credit hours, 24 credit hours are assigned for the course work and 26 credit hours are dedicated for research work.
  • What is the scheme of study? In order to know more about the scheme of study, click here
  • From where I can see the faculty profiles? In order to know more about faculty profile, click here
  • When is an MPhil supervisor assigned? MPhil supervisor is assigned after completion of course work (usually after two semesters).
  • What is future potential and professional growth after completing MPhil? Successful completion of MPhil at QAU opens many doors of professional positions both domestically and abroad in industry, academia and research.
