Research

Research in the department is carried out in research areas of faculty's interest through research groups.

  • Knowledge Software Engineering
  • Focuses on analysis of data, metadata and knowledge using supervised and unsupervised mining algorithms. The goal is to extract meaningful contextual knowledge for quality decision making.

  • This group investigates applied aspects in networking, communication, security and privacy. Focus areas include computer networks, distributed systems, and social network analysis.
